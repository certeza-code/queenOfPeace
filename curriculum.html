one school serving all!

Together we create a loving community of purposeful learning that focuses on the whole child; body ,mind and spirit.

our little angels

Our School is a city of little angels, smart brains where students are trained both in character and in learning.Education and morality are complimentary and inseperable because they contribute in the determining of the integral definition of a personality. Theodore Roosevelt say,“To educate a man in mind and not in morals is to educate a menace in the society”.On the otherhand, if the will is trained while the intellect is not, it will result in having ignorant men of goodwill. Therefore, we strive to nurture our girls in maintaining a striking balance between education and morality.

our school curricula

We ensure that our students follow a vast study programme, meticulously designed to meet the requirements of the National Curriculum of Nigeria.

senior secondary curriculum

Our senoir secondary is segmented into SS1,SS2 and SS3 where our students in SS1 offers the whole courses which include Mathematics, English language, Civic Education, Chemistry,Biology, Physics Agricultural Science, Literature,Government, Christian Religious Studies Animal Husbandry,Data Processing, Food and Nutrition,Economics, Igbo Language and Catholic Doctrine. Afterwards they write a promotional examination to the next segment (SS2 & SS3 respectively)where they choose their career paths( Arts and Sciences).

junior secondary curriculum

Our junior secondary is segmented into JSS1,JSS2 and JSS3 where they all offer the same courses but with different scheme of work viz: Mathematics, English Language, Basic Science, Basic Technology, Prevocational Studies, National Values, Christian Religious Studies, Igbo Language, Cultural and Creative Arts,History, French, Business Studies and Catholic Doctrines.

extra curricula activities

It is said that a healthy mind is found in a healthy body(Sana in corpora sano)hence our students are encouraged to participate in extracurricula activities such as:Interhouse sports, debates, football and quiz competition internally and externally. it is worth commending that our girls’ performance both in academics and extra-curricula activities are second to none.We also demonstrate our passion in ensuring our girls’ success in their career paths by estalishing different educational clubs for them such as :science/jet club, arts club,debate/press club, drama/cultural club, chorus club,and Queen of Peace club.We also train our girls’ in the path of moral conduct and good relationship with God through our pious/religious groups like block rosary crusade, legion of Mary,Catholic Charismatic Renewal Precious Blood.

our facilities

Our school facilities provide response to the changing programs of educational delivery and also provide a physical environment that is comfortable, safe,secure, accessible, well illuminated, well ventilated and aesthetically pleasing such as; Ultra modern hostel accommodation, Bore hole facilities, stand-by generating set in event of power failure, A 24-hour security survellience and protection, well ventilated classrooms,A standard science and computer laboratories, Student health centre unit, A peaceful and serene academic environment, Qualified and erudite teachers, High academic and moral standard,Affordable school and boarding fees, Excellent results(internal and external).

You will never regret sending your wards to us!
